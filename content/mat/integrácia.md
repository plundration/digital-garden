## Metódy
**Integration by parts:**
$$\int u \ dv=uv-\int v \ du$$

**U substitution:**
$$\int f(g(x))\ g'(x)\ dx = \int f(u)\ du$$
where $u=g(x)$ and $du=g'(x)\ dx$
