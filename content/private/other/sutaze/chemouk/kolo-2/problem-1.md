$$K_S(\ce{BaSO4}) = 1,1 \cdot 10^{-10} = c \cdot c \implies c = \sqrt{1,1 \cdot 10^{-10}} = 1.04 \cdot 10^{-5}$$
$$K_S(\ce{Ag3PO4}) = 1,8 \cdot 10^{-18} = (3c)^{3} \cdot c = 27c^{4} \implies c = \sqrt[4]{1,8 \cdot 10^{-18} / 27} = 1.60 \cdot 10^{-5}$$
